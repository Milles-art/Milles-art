About Us

We are a non-governmental organization that aims to improve the livelihoods of communities through the provision of education, health services, economic empowerment, and the promotion of human rights. Our foundation is dedicated to fulfilling the dreams of girls and empowering them to achieve their full potential.

Vision

A Tanzanian society where gender equality and inclusive economic growth are the norm.

Mission

1. To foster economic independence and ensure access to resources, creating a society free from all forms of violence.

2. To make sure that, the society observe human rights regardless of their gender and age.
3. To break the cycle of poverty and create economic opportunities to youth.
4. To create a society for youth to have to choose and create opportunities for themselves.
